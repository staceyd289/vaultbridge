    VaultBridge provides legitimate and secure assistance for people who’ve lost access to their WhatsApp accounts. We do not hack or bypass security systems. Instead, we guide you through official recovery steps and help you prepare what’s needed to reclaim your account.

    We walk you through WhatsApp’s official recovery process, including SIM retrieval, two-step verification reset, and verification code handling.

    We help you gather and format acceptable proof (ID, purchase receipts, backups) to support your recovery claim without violating privacy laws.

    Secure Submission Assistance

    We review your information and guide you on securely submitting it to WhatsApp support or your mobile carrier.

    Get a clear roadmap tailored to your situation, including safe communication tips and how to protect your account going forward.
