How It Works

We make the legitimate recovery process clear and straightforward. Here’s how VaultBridge helps you regain access to your WhatsApp account without compromising security.

1

Submit a Request

Fill out our secure online form with your details and describe your situation.

2

Verification & Guidance

We review your request and provide a checklist of what WhatsApp or your carrier will need for verification.

3

Document Preparation

We help you organize the necessary information or documents to support your legitimate recovery claim.

4

Official Submission

You securely submit the prepared information to the official support channels. We guide you every step of the way.

5

Ongoing Support

We remain available for follow-ups and tips to protect your account going forward.
