Safe Communication Tips

  • Use separate channels for sensitive info; avoid posting private links publicly.
  • Verify contacts through a second channel before responding to requests for money or data.
  • Prefer encrypted messaging and temporary links for sharing credentials — and then revoke access.
  • Keep records (screenshots, dates, and contact details) of suspicious messages for reporting.
